# Services

I work with teams to design and implement reliable infrastructure, automate operations, and scale applications.

---

## Network Architecture & Design

**Who it's for**: Teams building or scaling networks who need a solid foundation

**What you get**:
- Network assessment and design review
- High-availability architecture planning
- Migration strategy from legacy systems
- Documentation and runbooks
- Ongoing advisory (hourly or retainer)

**Deliverables**: Design document, architecture diagrams, cost analysis, implementation roadmap

**Pricing**: Starting at $2,500/week for projects; hourly rates available

---

## Virtualization & Infrastructure Automation

**Who it's for**: Teams managing physical servers or cloud infrastructure who want to reduce operational overhead

**What you get**:
- Virtualization platform selection and setup
- Infrastructure-as-Code implementation (Terraform, Ansible)
- Disaster recovery planning
- Performance optimization
- Operational training

**Deliverables**: Deployed infrastructure, IaC templates, runbooks, team training sessions

**Pricing**: Project-based (typically $5,000–$15,000) or retainer engagements

---

## Operations & Automation Strategy

**Who it's for**: Growing teams drowning in manual processes and firefighting

**What you get**:
- Operational audit (where is the toil?)
- Automation prioritization and roadmap
- CI/CD pipeline design and implementation
- Monitoring and alerting strategy
- Team coaching on DevOps practices

**Deliverables**: Automation roadmap, pipeline implementation, monitoring setup, team docs

**Pricing**: Hourly or retainer (typically 20–40 hrs/month ongoing)
