# About Just A Little Technical

## The Mission

Just A Little Technical helps small teams, creator-operators, and practical technologists make better technical decisions with calm, useful guidance that turns complexity into systems they can actually run.

## Who Am I?

I'm Mike, and I've spent years building infrastructure, automating operations, and debugging the systems that keep businesses running. I've learned that the most important technical decisions aren't about choosing the fanciest tool — they're about understanding tradeoffs and building things that don't require a PhD to operate.

## The Approach

The goal isn't more moving parts. It's fewer surprises.

I believe in:

- **Practical over perfect** — boring systems that run reliably beat clever systems that break at 2 a.m.
- **Documented tradeoffs** — every tool has costs; the best decision is an informed one
- **Plain English** — if it sounds like a keynote, we can say it simpler
- **Runbooks over heroics** — good automation reduces human drama, not replaces it

## What You'll Find Here

**Content**: Practical guides, tutorials, and tooling advice for infrastructure, automation, and operations

**Podcast**: Deep-dive conversations about technical decision-making, scaling operations, and the business of building

**Consulting**: Help designing systems for small teams who can't afford to be heroes

**Resources**: Free templates, checklists, and runbooks to get you started
