# Welcome to Just A Little Technical

## Episode Notes

This intro episode explains:
- **Why the show exists**: To talk about infrastructure and operations without the noise
- **Who it's for**: Small teams, creator-operators, and people building real systems
- **What's coming**: Practical interviews, deep dives on tools and patterns, and honest tradeoffs

## Show Format

Each week, we cover a topic that matters to people running infrastructure. We ask questions like:
- What actually happens when you scale?
- What breaks first, and how do you prepare?
- What did you learn the hard way?

We keep it simple, conversational, and useful.
